FDNY Commissioner Kavanagh Adds Names to Ever-Growing List of 9/11 Memorial

Somber ceremony invokes symbolism behind the looming milestone figure.

By Max Rivera

October 10, 2023

BROOKLYN, NY ––The names of 43 fallen members of the Fire Department were added to the World Trade Center memorial wall at FDNY headquarters on Wednesday. The FDNY members honored are part of an ever-growing group who sacrificed, or continue to sacrifice, after responding to the 9/11 terrorist attacks, said the FDNY commissioner, Laura Kavanagh.

She was joined at the ceremony by Mayor Eric Adams, high-ranking FDNY officials and family members.

List of fallen members expected to surpass 343 before end of year.
